Contact Us
Emergencies: If you are experiencing a life-threatening emergency, dial 9-1-1.If it is after hours and you would like to speak with a nurse, dial the main number (858) 534-3300 and select the option for the "Advice Nurse."
E-mail
We make every effort to respond in a timely manner, but it can sometimes take 3-5 days to do so. Use the links below to send us e-mail. 
Always write the word secure: in the subject line to protect your personal information.
Please do not send any personal or medical information through e-mail as it is not a secure method of communication.  If you are a registered student, use the links below to send a secure message.
Your comments and suggestions are important to us and we are committed to improving our services. Many past suggestions have contributed to new policies or procedures. All comments are reviewed by management, and patients who leave contact information will receive a personal response. ​

How Are We Doing? - Let us know your thoughts! Feedback can be submitted anonymously if you choose.  Use the link to submit your feedback via e-mail, or complete a comment card (available in our reception areas) in person.
