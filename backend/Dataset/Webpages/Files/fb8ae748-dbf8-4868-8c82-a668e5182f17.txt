SHS Invites your Feedback!
Comments and Suggestions 
Student Health Services and every member of our team is committed to maintaining the highest standards for patient satisfaction. As such, we encourage all comments and suggestions from patients and their concerned loved ones. You can submit feedback confidentially through our online survey, send an email to studenthealth@ucsd.edu, or call 858-822-4757.
Tell Us About Your Patient Care at SHS
To Provide General Feedback about your Patient Care Experience
SHS welcomes feedback on your experience. Please complete the following brief survey about your experience with SHS. You can choose to submit feedback anonymously, but if you include your contact information a member of the SHS team will contact you within 7 business days to let you know that we have received your feedback and what the next steps are. We appreciate your feedback.
