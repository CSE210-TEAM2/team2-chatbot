Online Scheduling
Online scheduling via MyStudentChart is now live. Students now have access to schedule for new and follow up non-urgent primary care and reproductive health appointments.*
Log in to MyStudentChart > Click on visits > Schedule an appointment > Choose SHS Primary Care or SHS Reproductive Health
Self-Scheduling Options Will Include:
Primary Care: skin, eye, urinary, STI, contraception consult/follow ups—all other reasons will be prompted to call for scheduling.
Reproductive Health: urinary, STI, vaginal, annual GYN, contraception consult/follow ups—all other reasons will be prompted to call for scheduling.

Please use your assigned primary health care provider (PCP) to ensure continuity of care.
The schedule will be available 30 days out at a time.

Please Call Your Provider Group to Schedule the Following:

Respiratory and urgent issues
Travel exams
Procedures
Video visits
Nutrition
Mental health
Social work
Sports medicine
Physical exams

You can call your assigned group directly or (858) 534-3300 to schedule these appointments.
*If you are unable to attend your scheduled appointment, please cancel or give at least a 4-hour notice to avoid a $20 fee.
