Sexual Health
On-Campus Resources
Student Health Services offers an array of services to serve students of all genders pertaining to their sexual health and well-being: 

STI Testing 
HIV Testing 
Reproductive Health Services 

Birth Control 
Breast Health 
Cervical Cancer Screening 
Emergency Contraception 


Gynecology problem visits 
Pregnancy Options 



Free safer sex products are available to students from The Condom Concierge. 
Student Health Services invites you to learn more about the following topics so you can make informed decisions when it comes to your sexual health:

Birth Control 
Safer Sex 
Sexually Transmitted Infections 
HIV 

Please find additional topics in the Resources Section.
